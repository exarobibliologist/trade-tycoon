# trade-tycoon
Trade Tycoon is a tycoon game (min-max buy low/sell high) game designed in Python. There are over 100 items to unlock, and a full prestige system so that every game is fun and entertaining.

# How to Play
###### (because someone requested a tutorial)

Trade Tycoon is an easy game to play, and requires no actual experience in the stock market to understand.
The basic rules in the game can be summarized as follows:
- Buy low
- Sell high
- Know when to use your artifacts
- Your score is what determines how much your prestige with. Go for the highest score you can

![Dominate the Market](https://github.com/exarobibliologist/trade-tycoon/blob/main/Dominate%20the%20Market.png?raw=true "Dominate the Market")

## Random
Everything in the game uses a cryptographic seed to provide randomness.

## Are There Cheats?
There is no way to futurecast the market anymore (the bug that allowed limited future-casting was patched a long time ago!)

## Items
These are what you trade throughout the game, unlock to reach higher levels, and after you have finally unlocked them all (thus winning the game) you can Prestige and start over with bonuses!

![Do Not Despise the Day of Small Beginnings](https://github.com/exarobibliologist/trade-tycoon/blob/main/Small%20Beginnings.png?raw=true "Do Not Despise the Day of Small Beginnings")

You start with 20 items unlocked, and 93 more to unlock!

## Artifacts
There are four overpowered artifacts in the game! You can only buy a max of 10 of them, when they show up in the market. They are expensive, but worth it!
Knowing when to use them is important

### Political Favors
![Political Favors](https://github.com/exarobibliologist/trade-tycoon/blob/main/Political%20Favors.png?raw=true)

Gives 10000 items for free. Useful for early game boost when a particular item price goes up but you don't have enough in your inventory to flex with.

### Owl-Chemist
![Owl-Chemist](https://github.com/exarobibliologist/trade-tycoon/blob/main/Owl-Chemist.png?raw=true)
Converts any item to any other item at 1:1. Good throughout the game because it's not limited to 10000 items. You can transfer massive stacks millions or billions of items between stacks.

### Smuggler's Writ
![](https://github.com/exarobibliologist/trade-tycoon/blob/main/Smugglers%20Writ.png?raw=true)

Let's you temporarily unlock a particular item for one week only and trade on it. This has a crazy loophole that I purposefully did not patch, and it is what makes the Smuggler's Writ so incredibly powerful! The Writ allows you to do more than just sell an item, once unlocked you can also buy the item. So if you have an item you got for free or very cheap, and can unlock it temporarily and buy it at that cheap price and max it out for later.

### Black Swan Catalyst
![Black Swan Catalyst](https://github.com/exarobibliologist/trade-tycoon/blob/main/Black%20Swan.png?raw=true "Black Swan Catalyst")

Useful at basically any point in the game. Crashes half the market to $1, the other half goes into to suborbital inflation. Using this artifact smartly, you create a massive tidal wave of inflation/delation and ride it for weeks.

## Prestige

After you have unlocked every item in the market, you can prestige. This:
- resets your game back to the beginning...
- locks all the items you unlocked
- You can choose to keep one stack of artifacts you collected.
- You get bonus starting money based on your score
